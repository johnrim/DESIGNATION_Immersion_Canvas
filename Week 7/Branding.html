Brief

Introduction of overall brand concepts: what is a brand? Why is it important?

Introduction to logos:
  • Why logo design is important: A logo is the encapsulation of the brand. Also, google 'why are logos important'. Color, feel, emotion, type should be consistent with your logo.
  • Best practices:
    • Type and iconography must be weighted as close as possible, or proportionately
    • Iconography must be extremely simple
    • If possible, if the concept allows, integrate the text and iconography as the same graphic (meaning some of the letters are part of the graphic itself, or the letters create the shape of the graphic they are saying)
    • Note: rules can be broken. For the right reasons.
  • Examples: Blog post of great logos
  • Design a logo for your cohort:
    • Sketch 5 logos for the same concept (your unique cohort) either by hand, digitally, or whatever you're most comfortable with
    • Present them, and let the class dictate which 2 are best
    • Refine the 2 logos in Adobe Illustrator
    • Do not be constrained with what DESIGNATION teaches in the cohort (no HTML/CSS/JS brackets) --> Analogy: no 'Stairway to Heaven' in a music shop
