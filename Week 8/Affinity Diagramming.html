Objectives & Outline

  2. Understand how to apply the technique to research

Brief

Affinity diagramming is a participatory method where concepts written on cards are sorted into related groups and sub-groups. The original intent of affinity diagramming was to help diagnose complicated problems by organizing qualitative data to reveal themes associated with the problems.

Brief

Affinity diagramming is a participatory method where concepts written on cards are sorted into related groups and sub-groups. The original intent of affinity diagramming was to help diagnose complicated problems by organizing qualitative data to reveal themes associated with the problems.

Exercise

Affinity diagramming simply consists of placing related items together.

  1. Write down research notes onto individual Post-Its
  2. Group Post-Its on the wall based on some theme or similarity
  3. Place headings next to groups of Post-Its
  4. Shuffle and reorganize the Post-Its as needed to find an overarching theme
